Facing eviction

If you are behind on rent or have received court papers, get help quickly. These NYC resources can connect you to free legal help and prevention services.

Immediate help

Right to Counsel (NYC) Free legal representation or advice for tenants in housing court. Call 311 and ask for Right to Counsel.

Tenant Helpline Call 311 and ask for the Tenant Helpline for triage and referrals.

NYC Tenant Resource Portal Guided help for eviction prevention, lockouts, repairs, and rent issues.

Housing legal aid resources if you need a lawyer or court help.

Emergency rent and utility help

One-Shot Deal (OSD) Emergency assistance to help pay rent arrears, utilities, or moving costs for eligible households.

NYC Rental Assistance Programs Overview of HRA rental assistance options.

HRA Utility Assistance Help for utility payments and arrears.

Con Edison Payment Assistance Payment plans and assistance options.

Arrears Supplement (ASAP) Help for rent arrears when available.

Court and legal guidance

HRA Legal Services for Tenants City-sponsored legal assistance and eviction defense.

Housing Court Answers Housing Court hotline, forms, and referrals.

NYC Housing Court Court locations, case types, and announcements.

Prevention and stabilization

Homebase Eviction prevention and housing stability services.

Homebase locations Find your nearest Homebase provider.

HRA Homelessness Prevention City programs to prevent shelter entry.
